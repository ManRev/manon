BeeGames

The BeeGames association was launched in 2015. We organized conferences about Management in Sports, featuring Pierre Dao, head coach of the French National team between 1975 and 1983, in different engineering schools. In June 2017, the first networking event took place, after a year of hard work with an amazing team, sponsored by Boris Diaw. Start-uppers, students and basketball officials met and networked on the basketball court. William Palmer organized the 2018 networking event. The BeeGames association was dissolved in 2019.
